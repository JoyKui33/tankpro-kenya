Our Process

How We Clean Your Tank

A proven 4-step process that ensures your tank is spotless and your water is safe to drink.

1

Inspection

We assess your tank's condition, size, and contamination level to determine the best approach.

2

Drain & Scrub

We safely drain the water and manually scrub all interior surfaces to remove buildup and deposits.

3

Disinfect

We apply WHO-approved disinfectants to kill all bacteria, viruses, and harmful microorganisms.

4

Refill & Report

We refill your tank with clean water and provide a detailed cleaning certificate and quality report.
